Biography

I’m an undergraduate student at the Department of Electrical Engineering, National Taiwan University. My research spanned areas in computational linguistics, signal processing, and computer vision, under the supervision of Prof. Lin-shan Lee (NTU EE), Prof. Hung-yi Lee (NTU EE), and Prof. Yu-chiang Frank Wang (NTU EE).

I am also an artificial intelligence intern in DeepHow, the first AI company bridging the skills gap in manufacturing, service and repair through an AI-powered knowledge capturing and training platform based on smart how-to videos. I am responsible for the AI algorithm development, which has been deployed to more than 40 Siemens service centers worldwide.
